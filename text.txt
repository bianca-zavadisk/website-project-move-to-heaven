Finding life in all that’s left behind, a detail-oriented trauma cleaner 
and his estranged uncle deliver untold stories of the departed to loved ones.

Ep 1
A factory intern’s fatal injury brings Han Jeong-u and Han Geu-ru to his room, 
where his goals for the future and love for his parents come into view.
Duration: 53m

Ep 2
Cho Sang-gu moves in as Geu-ru's legal guardian and new colleague. 
Geu-ru searches for a mother's message in her stash of cash and bank receipts.
Duration: 50m

Ep 3
A suit store owner reveals a mother's longstanding wish. 
Sang-gu is reminded of his costly promise. 
The trauma cleaners arrive at a bloody crime scene.
Duration: 46m

Ep 4
A user manual for a device unseen at the crime scene keeps Geu-ru up at night. 
Sang-gu must make a loan payment by putting up either cash or his fists.
Duration: 47m

Ep 5
The trauma cleaners search for the loved one of a slain doctor to 
hand-deliver a box full of regrets, hopes and an unrealized future for two.
Duration: 56m

Ep 6
Sang-gu and Geu-ru try to give a proper sendoff to a doting couple who called 
Move to Heaven after deciding to leave the world together hand in hand.
Duration: 54m

Ep 7
Sang-gu begrudgingly took Kim Su-cheol under his wing 10 years ago, 
unaware that his mentorship would bloom into brotherhood and end in destruction.
Duration: 44m

Ep 8
Sang-gu is coerced into signing up for a fight in exchange for Geu-ru 
and the house deed. On a special day, Geu-ru leads Sang-gu down memory lane.
Duration: 52m

Ep 9
Matthew Green's search for his birth mother ends with a 
heartbreaking misunderstanding. Sang-gu sets out to enter his final fight.
Duration: 55m

Ep 10
Not yet ready to let Jeong-u go, Geu-ru runs away from home. 
As their cohabitation comes to an end, Sang-gu’s qualification as a guardian is determined.
Duration: 62m
